As a child I was primarily interested in horses, not something you would expect someone technology interested to write? Well, this interest turned me to a website for horse-interested girls. In my teens I swapped out horse riding lessons for kickboxing, but I was still interested in digital art. I began to move on to other sites like deviantArt. After spending over a year at home I knew I wanted to do something else. I applied for Computer Science at NTNU. I spent my summer doing a precourse calculus, while looking after my toddler (not an easy task, as one can imagine).

Today

I am currently attending my 2nd year of Computer Science at NTNU Campus Ålesund, which focuses on application development. It has been very interesting and challenging, but also a lot of fun.
