About

Hello there! My name is Janita Lillevik Røyseth, I was born 1998 and come from Ålesund in Norway. For most of my life I've been interested in technology, digital art and design. As a result I am now currently attending my 2nd year at NTNU. I expect to be finished with my bachelors degree in June 2023.

Childhood

As a child I was primarily interested in horses, not something you would expect someone technology interested to write? Well, this interest turned me to a website for horse-interested girls. On this website one could design their profile using html, which is where I pinpoint that my interest for development started.

Teenage years

In my teens I swapped out horse riding lessons for kickboxing, but I was still active on the previously mentioned site, designing and drawing. But I also moved on to other sites like deviantArt. I later chose to study Media and Communication and ICT-Service, and continue with vocational education afterwards. I spent 2 years as an intern at the Norwegian Coastal Administration.

Adult life

After finishing my internship with the Norwegian Coastal Guard I found out I was pregnant. My plans took a completely different direction. After spending over a year at home I knew I wanted to do something else. So I applied for Computer Science at NTNU. I spent my summer doing a precourse calculus, while looking after my toddler (not an easy task, as one can imagine).

Today

I am currently studying the 2nd year of Computer Science at NTNU Ålesund, which focuses on application development. It is always interesting and challenging, and also a lot of fun. It has been a very rewarding road so far.

Course Work

IDATA1002 — Systemdevelopment

In systemdevelopment we had a semesterassignment where we were tasked with creating a todo-list for a specific group. Our group chose to make one for home care, to keep track of daily tasks, list of patients and more. We organized a test group of nurses of various ages and genders, and improved our system based on their feedback. We used gitlab to work together on the project.

IDATA2001 — Object-Oriented Programming 2

Because of corona, instead of one school exam we were given 3 projects over the semester in programming 2. First project we were given was to make a hospital system where we used inheritance by using interface and abstract classes. Second and third project were very similar in that we were tasked to make GUI for a patientregistry, and later postal codes in norway.

IDATA1001 — Object-Oriented Programming 1
